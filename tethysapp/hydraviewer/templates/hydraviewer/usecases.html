Myanmar Rahkine State Flooding July-August 2015

During the 2015 monsoon season Cyclone Komen made landfall in Bangladesh in July, bringing additional heavy rains into Myanmar. These rains lead to a significant increase in monsoonal flood water, leading to severe flooding and landslides. Of the areas affected, Rakhine State was among the hardest hit regions during this flood event.
